Join us for Aaron Swartz day 2018 from November 9th-11th Register Now

Why Attend

Internet Freedom Hack is a great way to apply your skills, network, and have fun working in a small team on meaningful problems. We make sure that everyone fits in to a team and has a good weekend.

Most hackathons and startup weekend events need to charge a fee but due to our generous sponsors and motivated volunteers, we are able to offer this event for free.
